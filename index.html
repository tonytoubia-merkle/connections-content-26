Catalog. Pricing. Inventory. Promotions. Orders. Payments. Everything the agent needs to actually transact — native, not integrated.

Most "AI commerce agents" sit on top of the storefront and pray. Ours runs inside it.

Why this maps to luxury portfolios.

A luxury group operates 10–30 brands across categories. Each has its own voice. Its own clienteling protocols. Its own customer.

The agentic question isn't "which brand goes first?" It's "can we run brand-specific agents on a shared substrate, with shared governance, that the holding company can audit?"

Williams-Sonoma is doing it now. The pattern is real.

The substrate

One customer profile. Any surface. Instantly.

The agent only works if it has the right context. Data Cloud unifies the customer across store, ecom, clienteling, service, loyalty, repair, regional preference — without ripping out your existing warehouse.

What it does.

Zero-copy federation. Snowflake stays Snowflake. Databricks stays Databricks. The agent gets one customer view; your IT team doesn't have a migration project.

Customer history, advisor relationship, regional preference, service notes — unified into a single profile the agent can ground every answer in.

Why luxury cares.

The boutique advisor on the iPad and the agent on the .com need to read from the same profile.

Same purchase history. Same advisor relationship. Same regional preference. Same service note.

Same trusted customer context. Any surface. Instantly.

Four things the Einstein Trust Layer does that other platforms don't.

01
Action attribution.
Every agent action ties to the human user who deployed it. Not a separate system identity. When compliance asks "who did this," the answer is a name on your team.
02
Dynamic grounding with sharing rules.
Agents see only what the user is authorized to see. Your CRM permission model extends to AI. No leakage across regions, brands, or VIP segments.
03
Brand-voice control + toxicity + prompt-injection defense.
Pre-screened content libraries for high-stakes responses. Real-time toxicity detection. Prompt-injection defense before the LLM call.
04
Zero data retention with LLM partners.
Your customer data does not train a third-party model. Ever.

And critically — guardrails are configurable by business users, not engineers. Your brand and compliance teams shape agent behavior in natural language. No IT tickets.
